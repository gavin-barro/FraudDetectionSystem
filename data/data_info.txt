This dataset contains credit card transactions made by European cardholders in the year 2023. It comprises over 550,000 records, and the data has been anonymized 
to protect the cardholders' identities. The primary objective of this dataset is to facilitate the development of fraud detection algorithms and models to identify 
potentially fraudulent transactions.

Kaggle: https://www.kaggle.com/datasets/nelgiriyewithana/credit-card-fraud-detection-dataset-2023

It should be noted that the csv from the link above was broken into 4 separate csv files in src/data/make_dataset.py as GitHub maxes out singular files at 100 MB,
the resulting 4 csv files in data/raw can be imported into 4 separate dataframes and combined for analyisis and model creation.
